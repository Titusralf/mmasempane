Mmasepane Primary School

Welcome to Mmasepane Primary School

At Mmasepane Primary School, we are committed to providing quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for a successful future. Our dedicated staff, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every child receives the attention and support they need to thrive.

Quality Education

Excellence in teaching and learning through innovative methods and dedicated educators.

Safe Environment

A secure and nurturing environment where students can learn and grow with confidence.

Holistic Development

Focusing on academic, social, emotional, and physical development of every child.

Community Partnership

Strong collaboration between school, parents, and the community for student success.

Our Curriculum & Subjects

Foundation Phase (Grades R-3)

▼
  • Home Language (e.g. English, isiZulu, Sepedi, Setswana, Afrikaans, etc.)
  • First Additional Language (introduced from Grade 1 or 2 in some schools)
  • Mathematics
  • Life Skills, which includes:
    • Beginning Knowledge (simple science & social studies)
    • Creative Arts (music, drama, visual arts)
    • Physical Education (PE)
    • Personal and Social Well-being

Intermediate Phase (Grades 4-6)

▼
  • Home Language
  • First Additional Language
  • Mathematics
  • Natural Sciences and Technology
  • Social Sciences (History and Geography)
  • Life Skills, which includes:
    • Creative Arts
    • Physical Education
    • Personal and Social Well-being

Senior Phase (Grade 7)

▼
  • Home Language
  • First Additional Language
  • Mathematics
  • Natural Sciences
  • Social Sciences (History and Geography)
  • Technology
  • Economic Management Sciences (EMS)
  • Life Orientation
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical Education

Foundation Phase

Building Strong Foundations for Lifelong Learning

Our Foundation Phase (Grades R-3) focuses on developing fundamental literacy, numeracy, and life skills in a nurturing environment.

Grade R

Play-based learning, social development, and early literacy and numeracy skills.

Grade 1

Reading readiness, basic writing skills, and fundamental mathematics.

Grade 2

Developing reading fluency, writing sentences, and building math confidence.

Grade 3

Consolidating literacy and numeracy skills in preparation for Intermediate Phase.

Intermediate Phase

Expanding Knowledge and Developing Skills

Our Intermediate Phase (Grades 4-6) builds on foundational skills while introducing more specialized subjects and independent learning.

Grade 4

Transition to subject-based learning with specialized teachers and more complex concepts.

Grade 5

Developing critical thinking skills and deeper understanding across all subjects.

Grade 6

Preparing for Senior Phase with advanced concepts and increased responsibility.

Senior Phase

Preparing for High School Excellence

Our Senior Phase (Grades 7) prepares students for high school with advanced curriculum and leadership opportunities.

Grade 7

Comprehensive preparation for high school with focus on academic excellence and leadership skills.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for the challenges of high school.

Leadership Development

Opportunities for student leadership, mentorship, and community involvement.
